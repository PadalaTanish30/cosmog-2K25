Frequently Asked

Who can participate?
All SMEC students; some events may allow external participants if announced.
Is there any registration fee?
No fee for entry; select workshops may require prior reservation due to limited seats.
Do I need a team?
Some events are solo; others allow teams. Check the event details.
Will certificates be provided?
Participation certificates for workshops; winners’ certificates for competitions.
What should I bring?
College ID, and laptop/charger if you’re in a workshop or coding event.
