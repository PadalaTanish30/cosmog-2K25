Code of Conduct

We are committed to a harassment-free experience for everyone.

Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our event a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

  • Demonstrating empathy and kindness
  • Being respectful of differing opinions
  • Accepting constructive feedback

Unacceptable behaviors include harassment, trolling, and advocating for or encouraging any such behavior.

Enforcement

Report issues to the organizing team at organizers@example.edu. Organizers may take appropriate action, including warnings or expulsion from the event without refund.
